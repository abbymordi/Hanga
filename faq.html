- How do I sell on Hanga?


-Hanga makes selling the items in your closet
easier than ever! Simply take a photo of the item
you want to sell with your device, add a quick
description, pick a price and you're done!

- How do I buy on Hanga?


-Hanga allows you to browse a wide variety of different fashions, brands and styles. Simply
search for your desired item, communicate with
the seller, meet up, pay and receive your item!

-Does Hanga take a percentage of my earnings?


-No! And that’s the best part. Unlike with many
other sites, every cent that you’ve earned from
selling items on Hanga is 100% yours!
